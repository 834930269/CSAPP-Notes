## Shift Operations

- Left Shift  x << y
    
    shift bit-vector x left y positions 
    
    - throw away bits on left
    - fill with 0's right
- Right Shift x >> y
    - Shift bit-vector x right y positions
        - Thorw away extra bits on right
    - Logical shift
        - Fill with 0's on left
    - Arithmetic shift
        - Replicate most significant bit on left
