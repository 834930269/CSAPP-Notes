Usigned number $u$ to its singed counterpart:
$$
U2T_w(u) =\left\{
\begin{aligned}
&u, &u \le TMax_w\\
&u - 2^w, & u > TMax_{w}
\end{aligned}
\right.
$$
$U2T_w(u) = -u_{w-1}2^w + u$
